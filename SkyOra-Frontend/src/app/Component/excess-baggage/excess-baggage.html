Weight Allowance

Each passenger receives a complimentary baggage allowance depending on fare type. Excess bags above the limit will incur additional fees.

Size Restrictions

Checked baggage must fit within the standard size limit. Oversize items may need special handling and incur extra charges.

Fee Schedule

  • First extra bag: moderate fee per segment
  • Second extra bag: higher fee per segment
  • Last-minute airport baggage purchase: premium rate

How to Save

Pre-book extra baggage during checkout for the best rate, pack efficiently, and review the allowance page before your flight.

Need help? Contact our customer support to find the optimal baggage plan for your travel itinerary.
