SkyOra Pet Policy

We welcome pets on board with care, comfort, and clear guidelines. Review the policy before you book to ensure your furry friend travels safely.

Pet Categories

Small pets may travel in-cabin in approved carriers. Larger pets will travel in the cargo hold under special handling conditions.

Booking Requirements

  • Reserve pet travel at least 72 hours before departure.
  • One pet carrier per passenger unless otherwise approved.
  • Carry valid health certificates and vaccination records.

Carrier Guidelines

Carriers must be leak-proof, well-ventilated, and fit under the seat for cabin travel. Soft-sided carriers are preferred for comfort.

Fees & Limits

Pet travel fees vary by route and carrier size. Fees are charged per segment and are subject to availability.

Health & Safety

All pets must be healthy and fit to fly. Owners are responsible for complying with local regulations, including quarantine and import/export rules.
